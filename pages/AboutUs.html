The Founders

Hello! We're the people bringing all these beautiful games to your computer. A lot of people have worked to make this website as beautiful and as useful as possible. Here you can read about them. I hope you will enjoy our work and feel free to contact us using our form.
Oh, and remember. Everything on this website is fictional, under all laws of this world. Any resemblance to any people, living or deceased, is purely accidental.

Ruxandra Mindru

Founder, Web-designer and literal Dragon

An aspiring filmmaker turned to Computer Science and the result is a mixture of cinematic creativity and tech-savvy. Still in her first year of University, Ruxandra (known on her Youtube channel as Sophie Proud) is a Romanian dragon, making games for everybody to enjoy. She likes climbing, dancing and writing about the ocean and the search for meaning.

Jonathan Hersh

Co-founder and Puzzle-wizard

You remember Ron Weasley from Harry Potter, right? Well, Jon is a non-ginger magician, bringing the toughest riddles of our time into small bite-sized chunks. Moving blocks, spot-the-difference, you name it, he can make it.

Rebecca Aberdeen

Web designer and Hero

Making a website is hard work, but the one that kept me and Jon alive was Becky. When we were stuck, she knew just what was wrong. A fairy godmother for all web-designers, she's now in charge of all the fun facts, the news, talking to you whenever trouble arises. If you receive a kind-hearted, lovely reply, she's the one writing it.
