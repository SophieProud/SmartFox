Accessibility

Ever since the idea of SmartFox came to life, we have wanted to make it accessible to as many children as possible. So, we did our best to make it easy to navigate for everybody.
The colours and text have plenty of contrast and the pictures have very distinct silhouettes and shapes, making for easy recognition.
Here is a list of the most important access keys.
Remember, it's Alt+Shift on Firefox.

  • Home: Alt + 1
  • News: Alt + 2
  • Games: Alt + 3
  • Smart Stuff: Alt + 4
  • Video: Alt + 5
  • About Us: Alt + 6
  • Contact: Alt + 7
  • Credits: Alt + 8
  • Accessibility: Alt + 9

All of our content is provided as both visual and audio and, since most of our videos are from the TED playlist, they always have transcripts for their talks. I hope that makes it easier for you all to access our website. If you have any suggestions on how to make it better or if we have missed something, let us know through our Contact page.
