Recommendations

There are millions of games online, some great, some very bad. Every week, we'll give you a few recommendations of what is worth playing.

Abzu

Never be afraid of the ocean again

One of the great games of this year, from the creators of "Journey" and "Flower".
Thatgamecompany has once again exceeded expectations with this underwater exploration of a gorgeous world, filled with animals you never knew existed. You can jump out of the water, swim with dolphins and even have fish follow you. An organic game, that will surely make you feel as if you're actually swimming.

FloodIt

Mind Games

If you're up for a challenge, this tiny game will definitely get your brain pumping for solutions. Try to complete the square with the same colour in under 25 moves. It's harder than it seems!
Play it here

The Great Escapes

3D Puzzles and Riddles

Not all cities have their own Escape Room, so if you have never went to a real life one, these games should fill in the gap. Solve the riddle, find the key, escape!

Monster: Survival of the Fittest

Escape the aliens

From SmartGames, here you have a clever game where you have to fight monsters. Have Fun!
